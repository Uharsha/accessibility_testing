Introduction to A11Y Insights

Welcome to A11Y Insights: Navigating Web Accessibility

A11Y Insights stands as a comprehensive resource hub for accessibility testing. Join us as we delve into the world of web accessibility testing, offering up-to-date content and insights to ensure inclusive digital experiences.

Educational Focus of A11Y Insights

Empowering Through Education: A11Y Insights' Mission

Embark on a journey of awareness and understanding with A11Y Insights, an educational website project. Focused on promoting web accessibility, particularly the WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines), our goal is to provide valuable insights for students and digital creators. Witness the evolution of this project as we strive to create a more inclusive digital landscape.

Origins at TTI Pune

Roots at TTI Pune: A11Y Insights' Inception

A11Y Insights was conceived by Siddharth and Ratinath during their time at the Technical Training Institute of PBMA (TTI) Pune. Through the institute's accessibility course, they dedicated themselves to promoting web accessibility. Now, A11Y Insights continues to provide valuable insights to testers and developers.

Overview

Welcome to a11y Insight, your go-to platform for comprehensive web accessibility testing. Our mission is to guide you through the principles, guidelines, and criteria of creating an accessible digital environment.

Services
